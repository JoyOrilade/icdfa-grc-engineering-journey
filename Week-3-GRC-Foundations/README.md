## Key Learnings & Highlights

- High-impact risks (even with lower likelihood) can receive higher priority than frequent low-impact events  
- ALE helps quantify expected yearly losses → guides security budget decisions  
- Insider threats often represent the highest financial risk when not properly controlled  
- Awareness training is one of the most cost-effective controls for human-related risks
